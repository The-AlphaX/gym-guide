DAY A — UPPER BODY
Do in this exact order · 3 sets · 10–12 reps · Rest 60–90 sec between sets
⚠️ One fix made: The original guide had no bicep exercise. That's a big gap — biceps are a key pulling muscle. Dumbbell Bicep Curls have been added as #8.
💪 Day A · Upper Body · 8 Exercises
EXERCISE 01
Lat Pulldown Machine
🎯 Back (Lats)
3 Sets 10–12 Reps 60–90s Rest
Sit → grab bar wide → pull to upper chest → release slowly. Don't lean too far back.
EXERCISE 02
Seated Cable Row
🎯 Back (Mid)
3 Sets 10–12 Reps 60–90s Rest
Sit upright → pull handle to belly → squeeze shoulder blades together → release slowly.
EXERCISE 03
Chest Press (Machine or Dumbbells)
🎯 Chest
3 Sets 10–12 Reps 60–90s Rest
Use the chest press machine first month. Push forward/up → lower slowly. Don't lock elbows at top.
EXERCISE 04
Incline Dumbbell Press
🎯 Upper Chest
3 Sets 10–12 Reps 60–90s Rest
Set bench to ~30–45°. Push dumbbells up and slightly inward → lower slowly. Keep feet flat on floor.
EXERCISE 05
Dumbbell Shoulder Press
🎯 Shoulders (Front + Side)
3 Sets 10–12 Reps 60–90s Rest
Sit on a bench with back support. Push dumbbells overhead → don't fully lock out → lower to ear level.
EXERCISE 06
Dumbbell Lateral Raise
🎯 Side Shoulders
3 Sets 12–15 Reps 60s Rest
Use very light weight. Raise arms sideways to shoulder height only → lower slowly. Slight forward lean is fine.
EXERCISE 07
Triceps Pushdown (Cable)
🎯 Triceps
3 Sets 12 Reps 60s Rest
Keep elbows pinned to your sides. Push bar/rope straight down → let it rise slowly. Only your forearms move.
EXERCISE 08
Dumbbell Bicep Curl Added
🎯 Biceps
3 Sets 10–12 Reps 60s Rest
Stand or sit. Curl both dumbbells up → squeeze at top → lower slowly. Keep elbows against your body — don't swing!
DAY B — LEGS + CORE
Do in this exact order · Follow individual set/rep counts below
🦵 Day B · Legs + Core · 6 Exercises
EXERCISE 01
Leg Press Machine
🎯 Quads, Glutes
4 Sets 10 Reps 90s Rest
Feet shoulder-width on platform → lower slowly until 90° → push through heels. Don't lock knees at top.
EXERCISE 02
Bodyweight Squat
🎯 Quads, Glutes, Core
3 Sets 12 Reps 60s Rest
Feet shoulder-width → sit back like lowering into a chair → knees track over toes → drive through heels to stand. Master this for 1 month before adding weight.
EXERCISE 03
Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift
🎯 Hamstrings, Glutes, Lower Back
3 Sets 10 Reps 90s Rest
Hold dumbbells in front → push hips back (not down!) → lower until you feel hamstring stretch → squeeze glutes to stand. Keep back straight throughout.
EXERCISE 04
Standing Calf Raises
🎯 Calves
4 Sets 15 Reps 60s Rest
Rise up on toes as high as possible → hold for 1 second at top → come down slowly. Do on a step edge for full range.
EXERCISE 05
Plank
🎯 Core, Abs
3 Sets 30–45s Hold 60s Rest
Elbows under shoulders → body in a straight line → squeeze core and glutes → breathe normally. Don't let hips sag or rise.
EXERCISE 06
Lying Leg Raises
🎯 Lower Abs
3 Sets 12 Reps 60s Rest
Lie flat on mat → raise legs to 90° → lower slowly without touching the floor. Keep lower back pressed into mat. Too easy? Try hanging from a bar.

Why incline walking?
Incline walking burns almost as many calories as jogging but with zero impact on your knees and joints. Perfect for beginners. It also works your glutes and hamstrings as a bonus.

FINAL REMINDERS

⚖️
Don't chase heavy weights. Light weight with perfect form builds more muscle.
🚶
